Daily out patient number of patients in the Health Centre is around 70 to 80 members. Daycare and admission of the patients depends upon the conditions. In case of emergency, we have the arrangement of shifting the patient to the higher centre for further treatment by our own ambulance.

2000

Resident medical Officer has been provided with quarters in the campus. He offers emergency services after the duty hours in the campus. About 1500 students reside in our campus Hostels and they receive the services from him.

1992

Health Centre is situated in our SIT Campus. It has started functioning from 5th October 1992. Health centre has two doctors and one staff nurse and male health worker and two supporting staff. Health centre has computerized patient data and drug store details.
